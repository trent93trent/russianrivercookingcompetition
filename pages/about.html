About the Russian River Cooking Competition

Where Culinary Excellence Meets Wine Country

Our History

The Russian River Cooking Competition began in 2020 as a small gathering of local chefs in Sonoma County. What started as a friendly competition among colleagues has grown into one of Northern California's premier culinary events, attracting talent from across the West Coast.

Named after the iconic Russian River that flows through the heart of wine country, our competition celebrates the rich agricultural bounty and culinary traditions of this unique region.

The Competition

Each year, chefs compete in a series of challenges that test their technical skills, creativity, and ability to highlight local ingredients. The competition spans three days and includes:

  • Mystery Basket Challenge: Chefs create dishes using surprise local ingredients
  • Wine Pairing Challenge: Creating the perfect dish to complement selected Russian River Valley wines
  • Signature Dish: Chefs present their personal culinary vision

Judging Process

Our evaluation platform allows judges to rate each dish across multiple criteria:

  • Taste & Flavor Profile
  • Technical Execution
  • Creativity & Innovation
  • Presentation
  • Use of Local Ingredients

Scores are tabulated in real-time, providing transparent and immediate feedback to competitors and audience members alike.

RRCC 2025

The 2025 competition will be our most ambitious yet, featuring:

  • 24 competing chefs from across California, Oregon, and Washington
  • A panel of 12 distinguished judges, including Michelin-starred chefs and food critics
  • Live demonstrations and tastings open to the public
  • A grand finale gala dinner showcasing the winning dishes

Join us June 12-14, 2025 in Healdsburg, California for this celebration of culinary artistry.
