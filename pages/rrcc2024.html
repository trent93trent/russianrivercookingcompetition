Winning Signature Dish

Pacific Halibut with Spicy Cilantro Rice

A perfectly executed dish featuring freshly caught Pacific halibut, expertly fried to achieve a golden, crispy exterior while maintaining tender, flaky flesh. The fish is served alongside aromatic spicy cilantro rice, creating a harmonious balance of textures and flavors. Paired with a 2021 Russian River Valley Chardonnay, this dish exemplifies our commitment to showcasing the finest local seafood with bold, complementary flavors.
