Russian River Cooking Competition 2024

A Celebration of Sonoma's Culinary Heritage

Competition Overview

The 2024 Russian River Cooking Competition took place on June 14-16, 2024 in Healdsburg, California. The event brought together 18 talented chefs from across the West Coast to showcase their culinary skills and creativity.

The competition featured three challenging rounds:

  • Mystery Basket Challenge: Using local seasonal ingredients
  • Wine Pairing Challenge: Creating dishes to complement Russian River Valley wines
  • Signature Dish: Each chef's personal culinary statement

2024 Champion

Chef Isabella Martinez

Harvest Table, St. Helena

"My cooking philosophy is simple: respect the ingredient, honor its origin, and let its true character shine. Winning this competition is a testament to the incredible producers and farmers of the Russian River Valley."

Winning Signature Dish

Sonoma Heritage Duck with Wild Mushroom Risotto and Cherry Reduction

A celebration of local ingredients featuring Liberty Farms duck, foraged mushrooms from the Russian River watershed, and Sonoma cherries, paired with a 2021 Russian River Valley Pinot Noir.

The Golden Spoon Award

Grand Prize Package

  • The coveted Golden Spoon Trophy, hand-crafted by local artisan metalworker James Ryder
  • $25,000 cash prize
  • Feature article in Wine Country Cuisine magazine
  • Culinary tour of Provence, France (10 days)
  • Opportunity to host a pop-up restaurant at the Healdsburg Food & Wine Center
  • Complete set of premium kitchen equipment from competition sponsor KitchenCraft

The Golden Spoon Award has become one of the most prestigious culinary honors in Northern California, launching winners into national recognition.
