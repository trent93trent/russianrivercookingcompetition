About Chef Justin

Justin Cuciz has established himself as one of Northern California's most innovative seafood chefs, with a passionate commitment to sustainable ocean-to-table cuisine. Born and raised in a coastal town, Justin developed a deep connection to the ocean and its bounty from an early age.

After training at the Culinary Institute of America and working in prestigious restaurants across the country, Justin returned to Northern California to develop his signature style that combines classic techniques with bold, unexpected flavors. His approach to cooking celebrates the natural flavors of fresh, locally-sourced ingredients while incorporating influences from his travels throughout Asia and the Mediterranean.

As the reigning champion of the Russian River Cooking Competition, Justin is known for his meticulous attention to detail, innovative flavor combinations, and artistic presentation. His winning dish in the 2024 competition showcased his ability to transform simple, sustainable ingredients into an extraordinary culinary experience.

When not in the kitchen, Justin can be found foraging along the coastline, working directly with local fishermen to source the freshest seafood, or teaching sustainable cooking classes to aspiring chefs.

Signature Dishes

  • Coastal Harmony
    Butter-poached local dungeness crab with sea urchin emulsion, pickled sea beans, and edible coastal flowers
  • Ocean Terroir
    Seared day-boat scallops with charred leek puree, crispy seaweed, and citrus-infused olive oil
  • Tidal Meditation
    Slow-cooked Pacific black cod with miso glaze, foraged mushrooms, and dashi broth
  • Coastal Forest
    Cedar-plank salmon with pine needle smoke, wild berry reduction, and forest herbs
