About Chef Marco

Marco Arballo is a culinary force known for his fearless approach to flavor and his ability to create dishes that leave a lasting impression. Born into a Mexican-Filipino family in San Francisco, Marco's multicultural upbringing is the foundation of his unique culinary perspective.

After graduating from the San Francisco Culinary Academy, Marco honed his skills in some of the city's most innovative kitchens before traveling extensively through Latin America and Southeast Asia. These journeys deeply influenced his cooking style, which combines traditional techniques with unexpected flavor combinations and modern presentations.

Marco's cuisine is characterized by its bold, unapologetic flavors, vibrant colors, and textural contrasts. He has a particular talent for balancing heat, acidity, and richness in ways that challenge and delight the palate. His dishes often feature house-made ferments, complex spice blends, and ingredients sourced from small, family-owned producers.

When he's not in the kitchen, Marco can be found exploring farmers markets, collaborating with local artisans, or teaching cooking classes that focus on building flavor through traditional preservation techniques.

Signature Dishes

  • Adobo Explosion
    48-hour braised pork belly with tamarind-adobo glaze, pickled green papaya, and crispy garlic chips
  • Fuego y Hielo
    Chile-rubbed grilled octopus with charred tomatillo salsa, avocado crema, and citrus-habanero oil
  • Umami Tsunami
    Miso-glazed short ribs with kimchi puree, crispy shallots, and fermented black bean sauce
  • Market Revolution
    Seasonal vegetable mosaic with house-made XO sauce, herb emulsions, and edible flowers
